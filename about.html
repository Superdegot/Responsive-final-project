Hi, I am Ekaterina Filatova

Swimming Teacher based in London, UK

If you want to know more about swimming or you are looking for a teacher to start your own swimming journey - you are in a right place. I'm happy to share my knowledge about swimming.

I'm a passionate swimming instructor with over 6 years experience. I spent all my childhood and youth in a pool, training every day. I reached the title «Candidate Master of Sports», participated in major regional competitions in Russia and won prizes. My favourite swimming style is breaststroke, 200 meters distance. The best time on this distance is 2:38 minutes.

I'm fully qualified by Swim England and STA. Never stop to learn something new and constantly participate in different CPD courses such as Birthlight, Aqua Sensory, Institute of Swimming.

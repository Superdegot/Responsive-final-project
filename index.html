What I do as a Swimming Teacher

Baby Swimming

When you introduce your baby to swimming early, it prepares them to be confident swimmers later in life. Swimming is a natural experience for babies, and my unique planned activities will achieve so much more than letting your baby splash about!

Swimming with Kids

All children are able to:

  • Develop a strong and confident relationship with water
  • Acquire technically effective, elegant and recognisable strokes
  • Build positive muscle-memory through slow-motion learning, retaining technique at any speed
  • Maintain total control and more safety in any marine environment
  • Swim beautifully and have some serious fun!

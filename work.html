Baby Swimming

Swimming strengthens their heart and lungs as well as their arm, leg and neck muscles. Bouncing and splashing in the water stimulates the vestibular system, aiding balance. Baby swimming is proven to help with little one's eating and sleeping patterns.



Swimming with Kids

As well as being an important life skill and teaching water safety, swimming is one of the most healthy and fun activities your child can do. It improves their strength, stamina, flexibility and posture, keeping your child active and happy at the same time.

Swimming with Adults

Swimming is a suitable exercise for people of all ages. It can help a person get or stay in shape, and the benefits also extend to mental health.
